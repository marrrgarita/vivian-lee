Lay Media Interviews/ Policy Publications

Ontario Autism Program Clinical Framework (June, 2017). As a research assistant for the Autism Spectrum Disorder Clinical Expert Committee and the Ministry of Children and Youth Services in Ontario, Canada, the report was prepared to provide a clinical framework for clinicians providing care to children and youth with Autism Spectrum Disorder in Ontario.

Fairchild Cantonese Fairchild Radio Show (AM1430) live radio interview (October 19, 2016; 12:00PM). Interview was with Jennifer Hong and was in regards to early diagnosis and interventions for children with autism spectrum disorder. The program was aimed towards disseminating information about autism within the Toronto-Chinese community in partnership with Autism Speaks Canada.

The Scott Thompson Show (AM900) live radio interview (September 27, 2016). Interview was with Scott Thompson and was in regards to the benefits of recreational activities such as therapeutic horseback riding for kids with developmental and intellectual disabilities

The Vancouver Sun news article (April 13, 2016). Article entitled “Opinion: The way towards kids’ brain health: Where is B.C.?” by Vivian Lee, Jonathan Lai, and the NeuroDevNet Trainee Policy and Advocacy Committee.
